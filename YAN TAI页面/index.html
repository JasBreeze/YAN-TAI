烟台冬季本土植物抗寒观察

A Study on Cold Resistance Observation of Native Plants in Yantai During Winter

Yantai, located in the northern coastal area of Shandong Province, has a temperate monsoon climate with cold and dry winters. Its native plants have evolved strong cold resistance after long-term adaptation. Therefore, the cold resistance observation focuses on their growth status and survival rate under low temperature conditions.
